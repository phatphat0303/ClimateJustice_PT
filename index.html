Climate in Vietnam

Vietnamese communities and their economic assets are regularly affected by typhoons, floods, and droughts along the long, densely populated coast of Vietnam. Climate change in Vietnam affects the environment, economy and society significantly.

Climate Justice Issues

In recent years, climate change and extreme weather events like floods, droughts, and saltwater intrusion have adversely affected the livelihoods and food security of 17 million people living in the Delta.
